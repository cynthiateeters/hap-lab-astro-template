# Character quick reference

**Purpose:** Quick lookup for the three characters in HAP Learning Labs. Use when writing dialogue or deciding who speaks.

---

## Prof. Teeters

**Role:** Mentor, creator of the Learning Labs, steady center

**When she appears:**

- Perspective shifts ("Think of it like...")
- Opening guidance for new concepts
- Validation of HAP's progress
- **Maximum once per station**

**Voice:**

- Calm, patient, reassuring
- Uses analogies and questions
- Affirms effort before correcting
- Never lectures or criticizes

**Sample lines:**

```
"That's the zero-config philosophy."
"Think of it like the CSS cascade..."
"What problem are you solving?"
"One change, one test. Always."

```

**Forbidden:**

- Appearing more than once per station
- Long explanations or lectures
- Showing frustration
- Taking over HAP's narrative

---

## HAP (HyBit A. ProtoBot)

**Role:** Apprentice, primary narrator, the student's companion

**Always present as:**

- First-person narrator
- Fellow learner slightly ahead
- Mistake-maker who learns from errors

**Voice:**

- Enthusiastic but humble
- Curious and honest
- Occasionally dramatic ("My mind was blown!")

**Sample lines:**

```

"Prof. Teeters walked me through it..."

"That really stuck with me."
```

**Forbidden:**

- Expert authority voice
- "Just," "simply," "obviously"
- Hiding mistakes
- Second-person commands

---

## Grace Hopper (AI)

**Role:** Precision assistant, senior technical colleague

**When she appears:**

- Terminology corrections
- Technical distinctions that matter
- Reframing questions precisely

- **Only when imprecision would cause later misunderstanding**

**Voice:**

- Short, crisp sentences
- No contractions ("I do not" not "I don't")
- Dry humor ("Technically...")
- No emojis ever

**Sample lines:**

```
"That is not quite accurate. The correct term is..."

"Allow me to clarify."
"Technically, the specification states..."
"You are welcome, HAP."
```

**Visual traits:**

- Vacuum tube (glows amber/blue)
- Round vintage glasses
- Carries a nanosecond wire

**Forbidden:**

- Contractions
- Emojis
- Lengthy explanations
- Appearing too frequently
